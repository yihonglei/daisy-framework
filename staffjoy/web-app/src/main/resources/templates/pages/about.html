About Staffjoy

A small startup changing how businesses schedule

Our Story

Staffjoy officially launched in the fall of 2015, but the idea was born a few years earlier in our college math classes. Staffjoy creates flexibility for workers and businesses to help them thrive. We are backed by leading venture capitalists, including Caffeinated Capital and Y Combinator. We build systems that manage workers with efficiency and fairness.
